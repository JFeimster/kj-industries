# K & J Industries Compliance Guide

This file documents copy and positioning guardrails for the K & J Industries funding access website.

## Core Disclosure

Use this disclosure in the footer and wherever appropriate:

```text
K & J Industries is not a lender and does not guarantee approvals, terms, funding amounts, or timelines. Services are referral and relationship-based through trusted funding partners.
```

## What K & J Industries Is

K & J Industries is a funding access and referral-oriented agency helping business owners start clearer funding conversations through trusted funding partners.

## What K & J Industries Is Not

K & J Industries is not:

- a direct lender
- a bank
- a credit repair company
- a guarantor of approvals
- a guarantor of rates, terms, amounts, or timelines

## Approved Copy Phrases

Preferred language:

- funding conversations
- funding access
- trusted funding partners
- funding readiness
- review path
- partner-fit conversation
- route the conversation
- may connect
- when appropriate
- business funding intake
- practical funding lane

## High-Risk Phrases to Avoid

Avoid:

- guaranteed approval
- guaranteed funding
- instant approval
- guaranteed same-day funding
- no documents required
- everyone qualifies
- lowest rates
- best rates
- direct lender
- lender network unless verified and approved
- pre-approved unless actually true and compliant
- funding guaranteed

## Same-Day Funding Language

Allowed:

```text
Same-day funding conversations
Fast review path
Funding conversations where speed may matter
```

Avoid:

```text
Guaranteed same-day funding
Get funded today guaranteed
Instant approval
```

## Partner Language

Allowed:

```text
Become a funding partner
Refer business owners
Help owners access clearer funding conversations
Relationship-first referral path
```

Avoid:

```text
Guaranteed commissions
Get rich as a broker
Everyone you refer will get funded
No experience needed to sell loans
```

## Naming Backend Partners

Do not publicly mention backend funding partners unless explicitly approved.

Use:

```text
trusted funding partners
```

## Review Checklist Before Publishing Copy

- Does it imply K & J directly funds deals?
- Does it guarantee an outcome?
- Does it promise speed, amount, approval, or terms?
- Does it name partners that should remain private?
- Does it overstate the current team size?
- Does it sound like a lender instead of a relationship-led funding access agency?

If yes, rewrite it.
